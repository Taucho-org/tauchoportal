About TauchoPortal

TauchoPortal is a live stream automation platform. It watches your YouTube, Twitch, and NicoNico channels around the clock, and when viewers interact — sending a Super Chat, gifting, cheering with Bits, or triggering emotion effects — your smart home devices respond instantly.

No scripts to run. No browser tab to keep open. The cloud service handles all detection and device control automatically.

🎯 How It Works

1
Add Watched Channels

Register your streaming account handles (YouTube, Twitch, NicoNico). The polling service detects when you go live automatically — nothing to trigger manually.

2
Define Event Conditions

For each channel, set rules tied to platform-specific events: comments, Super Chats, Bits, gifts, Nicoru reactions, hype trains, raids, and more.

3
Register Your Devices

Add your smart home devices — Govee, Philips Hue, LIFX, WLED, TP-Link Kasa, and others. Configure API credentials once; reuse devices across any number of conditions.

4
Stream and React

When a condition fires, the cloud service calls the device API directly. Lights change color, plugs switch, LEDs animate — in real time, while you stream.
